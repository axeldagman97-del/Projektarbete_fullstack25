FAQ

Do you have a physical shop?

No, we’re fully online. Orders are placed through Instagram Instagram or by email. You can however find us at pop-ups or markets from time to time, keep an eye out on upcoming events!

How do I order?

Send us a message on Instagram or email. with what you’d like. We’ll confirm your order and share payment and delivery details.

What if I have an allergy?

All cookies are baked in the same kitchen, using the same equipment. This means everything may contain traces of eggs, dairy, wheat, and nuts.

How long do the cookies stay fresh?

About 4-5 days in an airtight container.

How do I reheat them?

For that gooey texture: bake at 180°C (or 160°C fan) for 2-3 minutes. If frozen, 5-7 minutes after defrosting works best.

Can I freeze them?

Yes! Freeze to keep them longer, then reheat when you’re ready. They’ll taste freshly baked again.
